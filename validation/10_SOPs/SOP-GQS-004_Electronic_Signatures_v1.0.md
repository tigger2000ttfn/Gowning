## 1. PURPOSE
To define the use, meaning, and controls of electronic signatures in GQS so they are trustworthy and
equivalent to handwritten signatures (21 CFR Part 11 Subparts B & C: 11.50, 11.70, 11.100, 11.200,
11.300).

## 2. SCOPE
All electronic signatures executed in GQS, including the **trainer's attendance submission** signature
and the **QA qualification sign-off** (and any QA determination), which are bound to FORM-AST-36513 /
FORM-AST-36749 records.

## 3. RESPONSIBILITIES
- **Signers (Trainer, QA Reviewer):** execute signatures only for actions they are authorized and intend
  to certify; keep credentials confidential.
- **System Administrator:** ensures signature-capable functions are restricted to authorized roles.
- **Quality Assurance:** approves this SOP; reviews signature records during periodic review/audit.

## 4. DEFINITIONS
- **Electronic signature:** a computer record (identity + meaning + date/time) that the signer adopts as
  the legally binding equivalent of a handwritten signature.
- **Two-component signature:** signing executed with the signer's authenticated credentials plus an
  explicit certification of meaning.

## 5. PROCEDURE
### 5.1 Signing
1. To sign, the signer is **re-authenticated** at the point of signing.
2. The system records the **signer identity, date/time, and the meaning** of the signature (e.g.,
   "approved", "attendance certified").
3. The signature is **bound to the specific record** it certifies and cannot be excised, copied, or
   transferred to another record.

### 5.2 Meaning of signatures
- **Trainer attendance submission:** certifies that the recorded attendance for the session is accurate;
  routes attendees to QA review. (Prior to this signature, attendance is draft and no operator status
  changes.)
- **QA qualification sign-off:** certifies QA review and approval of the qualification; sets the person
  Qualified and is the basis of FORM-AST-36749.
- **QA determination:** certifies the disposition of a failed run (requalification scope / retraining).

### 5.3 Controls
1. Each signature is linked to a **unique individual account** (SOP-GQS-001); accounts are not shared.
2. Signature-capable actions are restricted by capability (e.g., QaApprove) to authorized users.
3. Signed records are protected from alteration; subsequent changes are controlled and audited
   (SOP-GQS-003), and where applicable require re-signing.
4. Identification-code/password controls follow 21 CFR 11.300 (SOP-GQS-001 §5.5).

### 5.4 Repudiation
Users acknowledge, per Astellas policy, that electronic signatures executed in GQS are the legally
binding equivalent of handwritten signatures and are not to be repudiated.

## 6. RECORDS
Electronic-signature records (signer, meaning, timestamp, bound record) retained with the signed record;
FORM-AST-36513 and FORM-AST-36749; related audit-trail entries.
