## 1. PURPOSE
To define how the GQS audit trail is reviewed so that creation, modification, and deletion of GMP records
remain attributable, complete, and trustworthy (21 CFR 11.10(e); ALCOA+).

## 2. SCOPE
The computer-generated audit trail covering GMP records in GQS (qualifications, runs, results, class
records, attendance, electronic signatures, and access/role changes).

## 3. RESPONSIBILITIES
- **Reviewer (QA / designated SME with ViewReports/Compliance access):** performs and documents audit-
  trail reviews.
- **System Owner:** ensures reviews occur at the defined cadence.
- **Quality Assurance:** approves this SOP; reviews findings; initiates investigations where needed.

## 4. DEFINITIONS
- **Audit trail:** secure, time-stamped electronic record of who made a change, when, and the previous
  value, not alterable by users.

## 5. PROCEDURE
### 5.1 Review cadence
1. **Routine (risk-based):** review of audit-trail entries for GMP-critical actions at the frequency
   defined with QA (e.g., periodically and at qualification record closure/QA sign-off).
2. **Event-driven:** review triggered by an investigation, deviation, suspected data-integrity issue, or
   inspection request.

### 5.2 What to review
1. Changes to qualification stage/status, run results and dispositions, attendance/class completion,
   due dates, and QA determinations.
2. Electronic-signature events (signer, meaning, timestamp, bound record).
3. Access and role/capability changes.
4. Any deletions/deactivations (confirming records were retained, not destroyed).

### 5.3 How to review
1. The reviewer accesses the audit trail via the application (Compliance / Audit Trail) and/or an export.
2. The reviewer confirms entries are complete and attributable, that prior values are retained on
   change, and that no entries are missing or anomalous.
3. The review is documented (scope, period covered, findings) and signed.

### 5.4 Findings
1. Discrepancies or suspected data-integrity issues are escalated to QA and handled under SOP-GQS-007
   (Incident/Deviation).
2. Corrective/preventive actions are tracked to closure.

## 6. RECORDS
Audit-trail review records (scope, period, findings, signature); related investigation/deviation records.
