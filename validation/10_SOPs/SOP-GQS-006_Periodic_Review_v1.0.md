## 1. PURPOSE
To confirm periodically that GQS remains in a validated, compliant, and fit-for-use state (GAMP 5
periodic review).

## 2. SCOPE
The GQS computerized system, its validation package, and the procedural controls governing it.

## 3. RESPONSIBILITIES
- **System Owner:** schedules and leads the review.
- **Quality Assurance:** approves this SOP; reviews and approves the periodic review report; determines
  re-validation needs.
- **IT / Developer:** provides change, backup/restore, and environment inputs.

## 4. PROCEDURE
### 4.1 Frequency
1. A periodic review is performed at the defined interval (e.g., annually) and may be triggered earlier
   by significant change or a major incident.

### 4.2 Review inputs
The review evaluates, since the last review:
1. **Change history** (SOP-GQS-005 records / `CHANGE_CONTROL_LOG.md` / release tags) and whether changes
   were properly assessed, tested, and documented.
2. **Deviations/incidents** (SOP-GQS-007) and their CAPA status.
3. **Access** — the current user list and access-review records (SOP-GQS-001).
4. **Audit-trail review** records (SOP-GQS-003).
5. **Backup/restore** test results (SOP-GQS-002) and BC/DR test results (SOP-GQS-008).
6. **Open risks / residual items** (RA-GQS-001) and whether prior gaps were closed.
7. **Document currency** — that URS/FS/DS/RTM reflect the deployed system (living-validation upkeep).
8. **Training** of users on the system and SOPs.

### 4.3 Output
1. A **periodic review report** documents the findings, the conclusion on the validated state, any
   actions/CAPAs, and whether re-validation (in part or full) is required.
2. The report is reviewed and approved by QA and the System Owner.

## 5. RECORDS
Periodic review reports and associated actions.
